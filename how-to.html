Space Race

Instructions

Welcome to a adventure in outer space!
When you press the "Start game" button you will have to choose witch spaceship you want to play as.
Then the game will start so be ready!

Play on a desktop: Use the keyboard Arrow LEFT and RIGHT to move your ship.
Play on a mobile device: Use your finger to move your ship, just press on the screen left or right.
Collect as many money bags that is falling around in space to get score.
But avoid the falling comets! That will end your space trip.
Good luck!
