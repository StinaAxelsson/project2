Space Race

Instructions

Welcome to a adventure in outer space.
First you will have to choose what spaceship to travel in.
When you have chosen the shapeship the game will start.
If you playing on a desktop, you move the spaceship with RIGHT and LEFT arrows.
If you playing on any touch device you just point the spacehip on the screen with you finger.
You have to pick up all the moneybags that flying around in the space to gain points. But watch out for the falling comets that coming towards you!
Good luck!
